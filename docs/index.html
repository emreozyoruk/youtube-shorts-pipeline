Seven pipeline stages. One button.

AI Script Writing

Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini writes hook-driven scripts optimized for your niche. DuckDuckGo research ensures factual accuracy.

DALL-E 3 Visuals

Three cinematic b-roll frames per video. Ken Burns zoom and pan animation. Visual style adapts to your content niche.

Voice Preview

300+ Edge TTS voices across 9 languages. Listen to any voice before generating. Switch between male, female, and regional accents.

Burned Subtitles

Whisper generates word-level timestamps. Subtitles are rendered directly into the video. No viewer action needed.

Auto-Detect Niche

Type any topic. GPT-4o-mini classifies it into the right niche. Script tone, visual style, music mood, and thumbnail strategy adapt automatically.

YouTube Upload

OAuth2 authenticated upload. Title, description, tags, captions, and thumbnail included. Choose public, private, or unlisted.

How it works

Seven stages, fully automated.

1

Research

DuckDuckGo searches your topic. Extracts facts, statistics, and context.

2

Script

AI writes a 60-second hook-driven script with niche-specific tone and CTA.

3

Images

DALL-E 3 generates 3 cinematic frames. Ken Burns animation applied.

4

Voiceover

Edge TTS records natural voiceover in your chosen language and voice.

5

Captions

Whisper transcribes with word-level timing. Burned directly into the video.

6

Assemble

ffmpeg and moviepy merge frames, audio, captions, and music into a 9:16 Short.

7

Upload

Published to YouTube with full metadata, thumbnail, and captions.
